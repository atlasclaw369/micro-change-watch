# Buyer personas and prospect queries

## Best first buyers

1. Indie SaaS founder tracking competitor pricing/features.
2. Consultant/agency operator monitoring client competitor pages.
3. Devtool/API-dependent team watching vendor changelogs/docs.
4. Launching founder who wants a 48-hour second pair of eyes.
5. OSS-commercial maintainer tracking dependencies/releases.
6. Newsletter/research operator tracking public product updates.
7. Product marketer watching competitor positioning pages.
8. Sales operator watching competitor plan limits and pricing.
9. No-code/automation consultant watching SaaS integration changes.
10. Small ecommerce/app founder watching marketplace listings/status pages.

## First-offer framing

Lead with the low-friction one-off:

> $10 one-off 48-hour watch on up to 5 public URLs. If useful, continue at $19/month for 10 URLs.

## Manual outreach guardrail

Only contact people/channels when:

- the context clearly matches monitoring pain;
- service/self-promotion is allowed;
- message is specific and non-spammy;
- no private credentials or sensitive monitoring requested.
